Fitness Equipments

CARDIO EQUIPMENTS

Treadmill

A machine designed for walking, jogging, and running indoors.

Stationary Bike

A stationary bicycle used for indoor cycling workouts that provide a low-impact cardio workout.

Jump Rope

A simple, portable cardio tool used for jumping exercises that improve coordination, agility, and cardiovascular health.

Mini Trampoline

A small, portable trampoline used for low-impact, high-intensity cardio workouts.s

STRENGTH TRAINING

Dumbbells

Small, handheld weights used for a wide variety of exercises targeting different muscle groups.

Barbells

A long metal bar with adjustable weights on either side, commonly used in weightlifting and powerlifting.

Kettlebells

A cast-iron weight with a handle, used for dynamic strength training exercises.

Bench Press

A weight bench with a barbell for performing bench presses to target the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

CORE AND STABILITY

Ab Wheel

A simple wheel with handles, used for roll-out exercises targeting the abs, obliques, and lower back.

Resistance Bands

Elastic bands that can be used to add resistance to core and stability exercises.

Medicine Balls

A weighted ball used for exercises that challenge both stability and core strength, including throws, twists, and slams.

Ab Crunch Machine

A machine designed to provide support during ab exercises, targeting the abdominal muscles for improved core strength.

FLEXIBILITY AND MOBILITY

Foam Roller

A cylindrical foam tool used for self-myofascial release, helping to release muscle tension and improve flexibility.

Yoga Mat

A cushioned mat used for yoga, stretching, and floor exercises to provide comfort and stability.

Speed Ladder

A flat ladder used for agility drills, helping to warm up muscles and increase flexibility through dynamic movements.

Balance Disc

An inflatable disc used to create instability while performing stretches or exercises that focus on joint mobility.

BODY WEIGHT TRAINING

Pull Up Bar

A bar mounted on a doorway or wall, used for bodyweight exercises like pull-ups, chin-ups, and hanging leg raises.

Push-Up Handles

Small, elevated handles that assist with push-ups, offering a deeper range of motion and reducing wrist strain.

Battle Ropes

Thick ropes used for dynamic full-body bodyweight exercises, involving waves, slams, and spirals.

Step Bench

A sturdy bench used for step-ups, lunges, and other lower body exercises using just body weight.
